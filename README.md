**The online AI education market has turned into a circus.** 90% of the "AI Experts" out there have one ultimate superpower: finding the "Sign Up" button on OpenAI's website. They peddle "Top 5 AI tools" PDFs and charge for masterclasses on generating pictures of cats. 

Meanwhile, real engineers are building zero-cost AI acquisition engines, orchestrating agentic workflows, and pushing terabytes of raw data. The real hardcore stuff is usually buried.

This monorepo is a protest against the infobiz graveyard. It’s an open-source kamikaze mission to drop non-obvious, production-grade knowledge about AI-Driven Growth, Agentic Workflows, and Data-Ops for everyone, for free. 

## 🏴‍☠️ The Manifesto & Core Principles

1. **Engineering over Infobiz:** We don't teach how to write "make it pretty" prompts. We build autonomous, production-ready systems.
2. **Hardcore Results Only:** No vanity metrics. No fluff. Just pure architecture, data operations, and scalable solutions that actually deliver.
3. **Open-Source Altruism:** For the short time we have left before the AGI coffee machines enslave us, we are making our most ruthless, efficient pipelines public.
4. **Data-Ops at the Core:** Treating LLM outputs as raw material that needs strict sanitization, routing, and structuring before it ever touches a database.
